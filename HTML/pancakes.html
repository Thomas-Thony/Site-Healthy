Hazelnut Flour Pancakes


To make this delicious dish , you need to have :

  1. One egg
  2. 60g of flour
  3. 60g of nut flour
  4. 25g of sugar
  5. 1 bag of chemical yeast
  6. 1 pinch of salt
  7. 120ml of milk

  • Combine the egg(s) and powdered sugar.
  • Then add the wheat flour, hazelnut flour and baking powder.
  • Mix well and add the milk.
  • Add the pinch of salt and mix to obtain a homogeneous dough.
  • Heat a greased frying pan and pour 1 tablespoon of batter into the hot frying pan for each pancake (you can bake several pancakes at a time, space the dough piles well).
  • Cook over medium heat for a few moments, turn each pancake very gently and cook on the other side before placing the pancakes on a small plate.
