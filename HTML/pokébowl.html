The pokébowl with smoked trout


To make this delicious dish , you need to have :

  1. Smoked trout
  2. Salad
  3. Basmatic Rice
  4. Carrots
  5. One spring onion
  6. White Beans
  7. Corail lentils
  8. Marined Ginger
  9. Mushroom
  10. Radish
  11. Mint
  12. Corriander
  13. sesame seeds
  14. Soy
  15. Soy sauce
  16. And sriracha sauce (or spicy sauce if you don't have this at home) .

You table has to look like this :



  • The first step is to peel the carrots .
  • Cut the spring onion into small pieces .
  • Grate carrots .
  • Cook the lentils and the white beans .
  • Every ingredient must be cut in little pieces .
  • Make the sauce by mixing the soy sauce , rice vinegar and sesame seed .
  • Put together all ingredient in a bowl .
  • Finish by cut little pieces of smoked trout .
