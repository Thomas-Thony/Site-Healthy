Almond King’s Gallettes


Ingredients :

  1. 125g of almond powder
  2. 125g of whey flour
  3. 60g of butterd butter
  4. 40g of apple sauce
  5. 40g of xylitol
  6. 25g of coconut bar
  7. 2 eggs
  8. 1 teaspoon of vegetable milk
  1. In a bowl, beat the two eggs into an omelet.
  2. Add xylitol sugar and coconut sugar.
  3. Mix well then add the apple sauce and the bitter almond extract.
  4. Stir in the ointment butter, then the almond powder and flour.
  5. Spread the dough with a spatula in a small mould (about 20/22cm).
    It is very sticky, which is normal.
  6. Don’t forget to hide a bean!
  7. With the back of a fork, draw the crossbones and
    brush with a little milk for the gilding.
  8. Bake in a 180° preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
