The split pea


  1. 500g of split pea
  2. 100g of bacon
  3. 1 onion
  4. 1 carrot
  5. Bunch of herbs (parsley, thyme, bay leaf)
  6. Salt and pepper
  1. Soak the split peas for 1 hour in cold water.
  2. Meanwhile, peel and slice the carrot into rings, and peel and finely slice the onion.
  3. Dip the drained split peas in a large pot of water.
    Add the onion, bacon, carrot and bunch of herbs.
    Bring to a boil and cook for about 1 h over medium heat.
  4. Drain the vegetables and bacon, keeping the cooking water and mash them with
    a vegetable mill or blender.
    Add cooking water to taste.
    This puree should remain thick enough. Salt if necessary.
